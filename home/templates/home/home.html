The London-based airline, #virginatlantic filed for #bankruptcy in the #unitedstates.

It seeks approval for a $1.5 billion recapitalization plan. The strategy includes debt restructuring for over 18 months. Under the plan, existing shareholders would contribute $750.6 million. #richardbranson 's Virgin Group would contribute $262 million and hedge fund Davidson Kempner would provide $188 million.
